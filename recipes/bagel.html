Anything Bagel


Description

How to eat bagel.


Ingredients

  • Water
  • Bagel
  • Oven / toaster oven
  • Whatever you want in your bagel

Instructions

  1. Leave your bagel whole and coat the entire exterior with a little water
  2. Put your whole bagel in your oven to toast for 5-6 minutes
  3. Prepare whatever you want to put in your bagel
  4. Once the exterior of the bagel feels crispy, take it out of the oven
  5. Put whatever you want in your bagel (maybe some ElsonLabs' egg salad)
  6. Enjoy.
