Ramen


Ramen is awesome!


Ingredients

  • Block of ramen
  • Water
  • Seasoning packet
  • Vegetable packet
  • Scallions

Instructions

  1. Boil water
  2. Cut scallions while water is boiling
  3. When water is bubbling, put in Ramen
  4. Add seasoning and mix intermitently while it cooks
  5. Put scallion whites at the bottom of the bowl
  6. Put ramen in bowl
  7. Put scallion greens on top
  8. Add whatever else you want
  9. Eat
