Egg Salad

It doesn't look like this lol.


Description

This is an ElsonLabs' patented "egg salad". Enjoy, or else.


Ingredients

  • Water
  • Eggs
  • Scallions
  • Cilantro
  • Avocado
  • Steak seasoning
  • Whatever else you want

Instructions

  1. Boil water
  2. Cut scallions and cilantro while water is boiling
  3. Poke a tiny hole in the bottom of the egg with a pin (optional)
  4. Gently place eggs into water (I use a strainer)
  5. Boil until yolks are jammy (usually 7-ish minutes)
  6. Cut avocado while eggs are boiling
  7. Take eggs out and peel
  8. Add scallions, cilantro, avocado, and whatever else you want
  9. Mash everything until desired consistency
  10. Add seasoning (can also season as you assemble)
  11. Mix to evenly season
  12. Enjoy.
